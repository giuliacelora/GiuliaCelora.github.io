I am a Research Fellow in the Mathematical Biology group at University College London (UCL). In my research I am interested in using continuous mathematical models to understand the spatio-temporal evolution of multiscale systems. Some examples of systems I have worked on are soft materials (hydrogels) and tumours. More recently, I have been focusing on biomolecular condesates and how the dynamics of these intracellular compartments is influenced by changes in the physical environment (e.g., changes in pH and salt concentration); this with the aim of gaining insight on the formation and time evolution of biomolecular condesated in cells exposed to stressfull environmental conditions (such as in cancer).
